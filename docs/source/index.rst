Welcome to OmniMind's Documentation!
===================================

OmniMind is a free, open-source Python library that simplifies Model Context Protocol (MCP) and AI integration. With a one-line setup, you can connect custom MCP servers to power your AI applications, leveraging Google Gemini for seamless workflows.

Why OmniMind?
-------------

- **Simple**: Add custom MCP servers with one line of code.
- **Flexible**: Build servers tailored to your needs, from APIs to databases.
- **Community-Driven**: Join our growing community on `GitHub <https://github.com/Techiral/OmniMind>`_ and Discord.
